Tips to reduce your food footprint

  • Choose locally sourced and seasonal foods
  • Reduce meat consumption, especially beef and lamb
  • Choose organic and fair-trade foods
  • Avoid processed foods and excess packaging
  • Reduce food waste by planning meals and using leftovers
  • Compost food scraps and yard waste

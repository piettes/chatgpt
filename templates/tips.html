Environment-Friendly Tips

Reduce energy consumption

  • Use energy-efficient light bulbs
  • Unplug electronics when not in use
  • Lower your thermostat by a few degrees

Eat more sustainably

  • Buy locally sourced produce
  • Eat less meat and dairy
  • Avoid over-packaged food items

Reduce transportation emissions

  • Use public transportation, bike, or walk instead of driving
  • Use an electric or hybrid vehicle
  • Carpool with others

Reduce waste

  • Recycle
  • Compost
  • Avoid single-use plastic items
